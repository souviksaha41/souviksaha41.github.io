About Me

I am a final-year PhD student at IMSc Chennai, advised by Prof. Saket Saurabh. Prior to this, I completed my BSc (Hons) in Mathematics and Computing at the Institute of Mathematics and Applications, Bhubaneswar.

My research interests include Parameterized Approximation Algorithms, Parameterized Complexity, and Graph Algorithms.
